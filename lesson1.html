Lesson 1 — Excel Interface

What is Excel Interface?

Microsoft Excel interface is the workspace where you create spreadsheets. It includes ribbon, cells, rows, columns, formula bar and worksheet area.

Understanding the interface is the first step to becoming an Excel expert.

Main Parts of Excel

  • Ribbon Menu
  • Quick Access Toolbar
  • Worksheet Area
  • Rows and Columns
  • Formula Bar
  • Status Bar
